# Product Overview

## Vision

Qatar's leading car wash service aggregator—connecting customers with professional vendors through convenient on-location vehicle cleaning services.

## Business Objectives

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Primary Market | Qatar (GCC expansion later) |
| Initial Scale | 30-100 transactions/day |
| Year 1 Scale | 100-200 transactions/day |
| Revenue Model | 5-10% commission |
| Break-even | 18 months |

## Platform Ecosystem

### Customer Mobile App (Flutter)
Primary interface for customers to discover providers, book services, and track appointments.

### Provider Admin Portal (Angular)
Web dashboard for car wash businesses to manage bookings, pricing, and customer relationships.

### Service Unit App (Angular PWA)
Mobile-optimized app for on-road service teams to receive assignments and update job status.

### System Admin Dashboard (Angular)
Internal tool for platform monitoring, user management, and operations oversight.

## Value Propositions

### For Customers

- On-demand services at your preferred location
- Real-time tracking of service progress
- Transparent pricing with local payment options (QNB, QIIB, digital wallets)
- Cultural timing integration (prayer schedules, weekend patterns)
- Verified provider network with ratings

### For Providers

- Access to broader customer base
- Efficient booking and route management
- Secure payment processing with automated payouts
- Business analytics and performance insights

## Market Context

### The Problem
Qatar residents face challenges accessing convenient car wash services:
- Fragmented market with limited digital solutions
- Reliance on word-of-mouth or physical searching
- Providers struggle with customer acquisition and scheduling

### Competitive Landscape
- **2 existing aggregators** in the Qatar market
- **Our differentiation**: Cultural integration, Arabic RTL, local payment gateways, prayer time scheduling

## Success Criteria

| Metric | Phase 1 | Phase 2 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Daily Transactions | 30-100 | 100-200 |
| Customer Retention | >60% monthly | >70% monthly |
| Provider Satisfaction | >4.0/5.0 | >4.2/5.0 |
| Booking Completion | >85% | >90% |
