# Product Overview

## Vision

Qatar's leading car wash service aggregator—connecting customers with professional vendors through convenient on-location vehicle cleaning services.

## Platform Ecosystem

### Customer Mobile App (Flutter)
Primary interface for customers to discover vendors, book services, and track appointments.

### Vendor Admin Portal (Angular)
Web dashboard for car wash businesses to manage bookings, pricing, service packages, and customer relationships.

### Service Unit App (Flutter)
Mobile app for on-road service teams to receive assigned bookings and update job status.

### System Admin Dashboard (Angular)
Internal tool for platform monitoring, user management, vendor approvals, and operations oversight.

## Value Propositions

### For Customers

- Book standard packages (Quick Wash, Shine Wash, Detail Wash) at fixed pricing
- Browse custom packages offered by individual vendors
- Location pinning with flexible scheduling (Now or Schedule)
- Status-based service tracking with estimated arrival times
- Local payment options (SkipCash, MyFatoorah, QNB, Visa, Google Pay, Apple Pay)
- Phone number registration with WhatsApp OTP verification
- Rate and review vendors after service completion
- SMS notifications for booking confirmation and status updates

### For Vendors

- Simple registration with business profile and document submission
- Manage standard and custom service packages with individual pricing
- Accept or reject booking requests with real-time notifications
- Manage multiple on-road service units (fleet assignment)
- Earnings dashboard with commission tracking and transaction history
- Toggle availability per service or go offline when needed

## Success Criteria

| Metric | Phase 1 | Phase 2 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Daily Transactions | 30-100 | 100-200 |
| Customer Retention | >60% monthly | >70% monthly |
| Vendor Satisfaction | >4.0/5.0 | >4.2/5.0 |
| Booking Completion | >85% | >90% |
